The best way to get started with Violet is to work your way through the following checklist. You will sign up for a Violet account, create an application and test API calls through our Postman collection; exposing you to all the APIs and data you can use to solve a variety of problems. 

## Quickstart Checklist

1. Signup at channel.violet.io and [Create an App](/guides/quickstart/create-a-violet-application)  (5 minutes)
2. Download our [Postman Collection](https://app.getpostman.com/run-collection/17013834-8291b096-4ce6-4b24-8723-f097affd1cc1?action=collection%2Ffork&collection-url=entityId%3D17013834-8291b096-4ce6-4b24-8723-f097affd1cc1%26entityType%3Dcollection%26workspaceId%3Dd1ce76c5-0538-4b7c-bcc5-4ee788f1e7dd) and login to your application [Postman + Login](/guides/quickstart/postman-login)  (2 minutes)
3. Navigate through our Merchant APIs at [View Merchants](/guides/quickstart/view-merchants)  (15 minutes)
4. Navigate through our Product catalog and select products [Interact with Catalogs](/guides/quickstart/interact-with-catalogs) (15 minutes)
5. Work through the order and checkout process by [Placing an Order](/guides/quickstart/place-an-order)  (45 minutes)
